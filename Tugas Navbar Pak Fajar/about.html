About myself

I aspire to be a backend programmer, database specialist, and AI/ML engineer. I also aspire to be a polymath because I have a strong curiosity for all knowledge and am devoutly religious.

And I want to contribute to education in Indonesia by opening a science academy. Why did I choose a science academy? Because I believe Indonesia needs more generations who master science. This knowledge is not only the foundation for academic progress, but also the key to developing the nation's future infrastructure and technology. Furthermore, science is also a unique and profound way to learn about and admire God's creation.
